How it works

Three steps, zero friction.

No accounts, no cloud storage of your prompts, no leaving the page you're already on.

01

Write your rough idea

Type into Claude, ChatGPT, or Gemini like you normally would — half-formed thoughts included.

02

Hit the spark

Forge sends it to Groq's Llama 3.3 70B, which restructures it for the exact platform you're on.

03

Review, then send

Preview the rewrite, tweak anything, accept. Nothing sends automatically — you're always in control.

Why Forge

Built to stay out of your way.

Genuinely free

Open chrome://extensions and enable Developer mode (top right).
Click Load unpacked and select the unzipped folder (the one containing manifest.json).
Click the Forge icon in your toolbar, paste a free key from console.groq.com, and save.
